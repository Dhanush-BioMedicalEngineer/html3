The Universe:

Some of the earliest cosmological models of the universe were developed by ancient Greek and Indian philosophers and were geocentric,placing Earth at the center.
Over the centuries, more precise astronomical observations led Nicolaus Copernicus to develop the heliocentric model with the Sun at the center of the Solar System.


Our Solar System:

The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it.
It was formed about 4.6 billion years ago when a dense region of a molecular cloud collapsed, forming the Sun and a protoplanetary disc.
